Nebius has partnered with NVIDIA to deploy two retail AI blueprints on its infrastructure: the Agentic Commerce Blueprint and the Retail Catalog Enrichment Blueprint. According to NVIDIA's 2026 survey, 47% of retailers are exploring agentic AI and 42% are investing in catalog enrichment, though only 20% have moved beyond pilots. Nebius provides the GPU infrastructure and managed inference capabilities needed to run these open-source reference architectures at production scale.
